Saturn Transit 2020 In Capricorn Sign: Effects & Predictions For Sagittarius
Nakshatras: Moola, Poorvaashada, Uttaraashada

For Sagittarius Saturn is lord of the 2nd and 3rd house and is a malefic planet for them, if afflicted in natal Horoscope, it can cause severe issues in life.

Generally, Saturn rules over following aspects for Sagittarius natives:

  • Salary.
  • Capability for earnings.
  • Speech.
  • Food Habits.
  • Talking abilities.
  • Family values.
  • Courage.
  • Strength.
  • Grit & Determination.
  • Siblings.

During year 2020 Saturn will aspect 4th, 8th and 11th house respectively and accordingly these house and planets in them in natal Horoscope will be affected by it.

Let's see in detail how various areas of their life will be affected by this Transit of Saturn in Capricorn 2020.

CAREER

So far Saturn was in sign of Sagittarius and was casting a aspect on the 10th house causing delays and obstacles in career progress, now this new movement of Saturn will lead to good effects for you.

If you are salaried or in service sector then this Transit will lead to better growth and money flow, chances of promotion or salary hike is not denied and your efforts form the past will start to yield results for you.

There can be a transfer for you and you may find a place of your liking so a positive transit for career is seen.

If you are in Business or makes money via speculations then this Transit will provide you to break the slowdown which you have been experiencing for a long time.

You will be able to expand your business and can make some more investments and you will be able to witness gains from January 2020, this year will lead to some positive events at your business and speculations is also going to suit you very well. 

WEALTH

Since Saturn transit in Capricorn will be going through your 2nd house in own sign, I am sure that your finances will start to pick up now.

As evident in Career section that your career will see positive lights now and accordingly your wealth will also.

Though, Saturn transit in Capricorn is going to be best for those who are in services sector like job. For business person's Saturn transit in Aquarius will be more beneficial than this one, still you can expect more gains in this transit but remember next Saturn transit will be the best transit for you.

RELATIONSHIP

Again, a being relief for you is coming in relationships because negative aspect of Saturn over the 7th house won't be present now.

Rather, in own sign in second house Saturn will lead to better times with spouse and family.

Though, there can be times when you will miss your family due to physical distance, this may happen due to work responsibilities and duties.

If you are unmarried then this Transit shows show hopes for Marriage for you and I think with support of Jupiter transit in 1st house you will be able to settles down in end phases of year 2020.

If you are planning for a kid then again a supportive indication is seen and you will be able to become a parent in year 2020.

Relationship with parents and siblings will improve further and the difficulties which your Father is experiencing for quite a long time will start to vanish now.

From Mother point of view I would say that some attention is needed on her health but nothing serious will come just some minor issues which will settles down with time.

HEALTH

From health point of view I think this transition of Saturn can revive you, the laziness and confusion which you were experiencing so far will start to go away.

There will be a new energy level in you and any ongoing health issue will find a cure though it is suggest you to watch over your diet and food intake and if you have habit of smoking or drinking then try to reduce it as much as possible because naturally in 2nd house will make you indulge into these things more and more.
