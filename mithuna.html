Saturn Transit 2020 In Capricorn Sign: Effects & Predictions For Gemini
Nakshatras: Mrigashira, Ardra, Punarvasu

For Gemini Saturn is lord of the 8th & 9th house and have tricky lordship. You need to carefully judge the strength of planet Saturn.

A weak Saturn can lead to negative effects of the 8th house and will lead to some serious situations for the native.

During 2020 Saturn will transit over the 8th house and will aspect 10th, 2nd and 5th house respectively, this Transit of Saturn can be complex and may bring interesting twists in natives life.

Let's see in detail how Saturn will affect Gemini in year 2020.

CAREER

Saturn will be aspecting 10th house from the 8th house, this aspect is not looking well and can affect your career growth and may suddenly cause some disturbance especially when you are in Dasha of Mars, afflicted Moon or Ketu.

There can be sudden changes in your career and your position may get reduced to low capacity and same time workplace issues can bother you a lot. There can be hidden enemies at workplace who can bother your peace of mind and even friends can backstab you out of nowhere.

You need to be careful in this regard  because possibilities of having issues at workplace is very high for you. Don't take any important decisions in your life and for two years i.e 2021 & 2020 you need to stay low.

If you are in Business than Saturn transit 2020 is going to bring unnecessary issues at Business and cash flow will be stuck for you, you should not take any chance with monetary decisions and avoid to take risks at any cost.

WEALTH

Despite career issues, this Saturn transit 2020 have some bright areas from where you are likely to gain good gains especially in form of inheritance or sudden gains from the investments from the past.

Though still you should be carefull with the money part and don't make any important decisions in your life like any kind of investment is not advisable at all.

RELATIONSHIP

This new transit of Saturn from the 8th house will be 2nd from your 7th house of marriage and even though it will be own sign of Capricorn, I think still you should be careful of your equation with your spouse.

There can be situations where things may get tense between you two and you should try not to use bad language because Saturn will have a direct aspect on your 2nd house of speech which can lead to issues with the quality of words you will be choosing in a argument.

With parents things are looking ok and I don't see issues with either Father or Mother. Sibling on other hand can pose some issues especially in getting inheritance and there can be some arguments and relationship with sibling may not remain the same.

There are some chances of child birth in your case and there will be happiness through children's, they may perform well in studies and if they are grown ups then their career will see positive direction.

HEALTH 

Presence of Saturn in your 8th house is not that great for health especially in year 2021 when Jupiter which is a maraka is going to be debilitated, apart from that everything looks ok and you will be able to recover very fast.

REMEDIES

The best way to deal with Saturn presence in your 8th house is via making your strong via a Jyotish quality Peridot on pendant or on smallest finger of right hand. This will allow you relief from the negative effects of it.
