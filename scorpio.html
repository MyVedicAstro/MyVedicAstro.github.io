Saturn Transit 2020 In Capricorn Sign: Effects & Predictions For Scorpio
Nakshatras: Vishaka, Anuradha, Jyesta

Saturn is lord of 3rd and 4th house here and is a malefic planet for them. Saturn relationship with Mercury in Natal chart can create havoc in natives life.

Scorpio sign natives should definitely take Saturn seriously and they can not ignore this Transit in Capricorn during 2020.

Saturn will be transiting through 3rd house which signifies courage, will power, travels, strength etc and Saturn will aspect on 5th, 9th and 2nd house respectively during this Transit.

This means, Saturn transit in Capricorn is going to be better than previous years and the malefic results will be lesser than before.

Let's see in detail how it is going to affect them.

The new found energy and hunger for success will keep you going ahead and you will be making plans for gains and will be looking for new opportunities. Issues at workplace will be handled well by you and you will be able to break free from enemies.

If you are looking for a job then you will have it by March 2020, if there are issues at workplace then you will be able to handle them smartly.

Similarly, a chance to go abroad may come at your steps and you will be benefited by it immensely, if you are in business, sports or in politics then this transit have ability to give a rise.

Whereas if you are in stocks or any kind of speculations then you should think and plan before making any decision on investments naturally you will be thinking a lot and may want to take guidance from experienced people around you and this may prove a excellent decision for you.

Overall a excellent transit to make your career better and you surely don't want to miss it.

WEALTH

But despite of all the restrictions, still you will be making much better money then previous years and same time your expenses will also be high especially on traveling and hobbies, this will be a excellent time to spend on your self development and it is like a investment which will lead to gains in future for you.

RELATIONSHIP
