Saturn Transit 2020 In Capricorn Sign: Effects & Predictions For Cancer
Nakshatras: Punarvasu, Pushya, Ashlesha

Saturn is most malefic planet for Cancer sign since it rules over 7th and 8th house, even a slight affliction on Saturn from Mercury, Ketu or Rahu can make Saturn very dangerous.

Saturn signify death, bad health, longevity, inheritance, marriage, partnerships, features of the spouse etc.

During Saturn transit 2020 in Capricorn sign Saturn will be transit over their 7th house and will aspect 1st house, 9th and 4th house respectively. 

This Saturn Transit 2020 is going to be interesting one and let's see how it will affect various areas of their life.

CAREER

Technically speaking this transit of Saturn isn't that great and may cause some issues. There will be situations where you will be seeing your enemies on the rise and suddenly you will start to attract attention for negative things.

You need to be careful about what you are doing and what you are saying. Best is to mind your own business and let this transit settle down for you.

There can be abroad opportunities for you after a hard fought battle among your competitors and this is the best aspect of this Transit, though you need to have such combinations in your Horoscope which makes one travel abroad for work.

If you are employed then a good chance is there that you will be able to crack a exam and land a job for yourself.

Though, if Business is your main source of income then this transit is indicating that you may take one loans for raw material or expansion of Business, try  not to go overboard with your debts and keep a check on people who works for you.

This transit will prove best for sportsmen and politicians mainly, lawyers are also likely to get benefited by it, overall Saturn Transit 2020 is not bad for you, just some precautions and wisdom is required on your part.

WEALTH

Direct aspect of Saturn over the 4th house shows the need of loans in year 2020 and this may lead to good results because your investment will lead gains for you, still I suggest you to control few things in your lifestyle and done go overboard with expenses.

RELATIONSHIPS

Saturn transit 2020 shows some issues at Marriage front and there can be more delays in finding a partner for yourself ,but have patience because 2021 will bring a good news for you.

If you are already married and then you can expect some difference in opinion with spouse, though they won't be large unless you have severe negative combinations for bad Marriage.

With parents and siblings this Transit will cause some drifts and you may have some arguments with your father, mother is going to remain supportive but still you need to understand and respect them as much as possible. 

With siblings this transit don't have much impact and their life will be same like before. I don't see much changes in year 2020.

HEALTH

Your health is going to see improvements and you may start going to gym now. There will be focus on improve your appearance and you will be giving your best in it.

Any ongoing health issue will find its cure but not right away, you do need to give your best efforts for this, naturally some expenditures are going to occur on account on health but at least you will be able to find the cure.

REMEDIES

Since this Transit of Saturn is not that supportive, you should read Shri Vishnu Sahasranamam whenever possible, this will help you to deal with the slowness of this transit in year 2020.

Wear a Jyotish quality Red Coral as a remedy to minimize the bad effects of this transit of Saturn.
